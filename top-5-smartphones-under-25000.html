Time of budget smartphones

2020 is turning out to be a year for many mid-range smartphones under Rs 25,000. so if you are looking to buy the best smartphone under Rs 25,000 this is the right time for you.

Nowadays many of the budget smartphones are providing more powerful SoCs from manufacturers such as Qualcomm and MediaTek. The 2020 budget smartphones now offer almost all the features of a high-end smartphone which was just a dream for many budget buyers.
